OUR ROOTS.

Everybody has a story to tell we do too!!!

GETSETWHEELS.COM is the first chapter/product from ALPHANUMERIC TECHNOLOGY SOLUTIONS(ANTS) stable. GETSETWHEELS is an online aggregator for rental vehicles currently providing services in Mumbai and Pune. It provides a platform to the customers to rent vehicles of their choice and make hassle free online payment.

GETSETWHEELS connects customers to their vehicles seamlessly at most affordable daily rates and Rs.0 security deposit. GETSETWHEELS provides both Bikes and Cars rental services and is your one stop shop for rental wheels.

OUR STRENGTHS.

Seamless Connectivity

GSW mission is to provide a seamless connectivity to our customers to the vehicles of their choice either a two or four-wheeler with a hassle free and trusted online payment system. To enhance the customers overall trip experience and ensure the comfort of each user.

GSW provides the customer with a wide variety of vehicles choices to make, decreasing the customer’s difficulty in search of right vehicles.

GSW ensures that journey of our customers is memorable one and we are happy to play our small role in it….

OUR GROWTH AND VISION.

To provide every Indian with their wheels

GSW vision is to solve the problem of vehicle renting in India and soon plans to expand its operations in other metros as well as other Tier-2 and Tier-3 cities.

Our aim is to provide hassle free renting experiences to our customers either for daily commuting to offices or planning for a big road trip with your friends and family.

GSW does not charge surcharges or a complicated tariff structure with hidden costs, our rates are strictly for per day basis and are most affordable across the board, ditch the cabs and for people who like to drive rent your wheels.

GSW is here to provide you with your wings #getyourwheels……
