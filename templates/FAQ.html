Frequently Asked Questions

  • How to reserve a bike ?

Select the bike you like from our given list of available bikes and also select the pickup location of your choice(i.e near by location). You will be asked to login or sign up. Sign up is easy with few essential details like email address and phone number. After that, reconfirm your selected bike, dates and pickup location and you will be taken to the payment gateway. Your reservation is confirmed after you make the payment and you will get a message from our website

  • Is there any speed limit for the bike?

Yes, for bikes below 350cc 80km/hr is the limit. for bikes above 350cc 100km/hr is the limit. if you exceed the speed limit, then you will be fined for 500rs.

  • What are the cancellation policy?

1. 0-24 Hours before start of the trip – The entire amount paid by the customer will be charged as cancellation fee. There will be NO REFUND made for such cancellations.
2. More than 24 hours before start of the trip – 20% of the amount paid by the customer will be reduced as cancellation fee and the remaining 80% of the amount will be refunded. The refunded amount will reflect in the customers bank account within 5-7 business days.
3. If any booking is cancelled by GETSETWHEELS for any reason, a full refund will be initiated
